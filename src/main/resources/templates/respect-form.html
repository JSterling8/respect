Huddersfield & District Football Association Referee Report Form

Following some concerns raised at the November Referees' Association Meeting regarding the operation of the new online respect card, please ensure you read the below instructions carefully and thoroughly.

Please ensure that you select the correct League, for district league: division 1 - reserve division 4, for works league: division 1 - division 2, Inter section competition & Arthur Coupland trophy section A or B, and Kirklees Sunday league: Division 1. For any of the district cup competitions, please select "Huddersfield FA" under the league drop down, and select the correct cup from the competition drop down.

ALL teams are included in the drop down menus, including reserve, 'A' & 'B' teams. If you are officiating on a reserve division fixture, please ensure you select the correct reserve, 'A' or 'B' teams rather than the first team. For Works league fixtures, all teams are prefixed by 'WL' and can be found at the bottom of the drop down menu.

Where clubs have a district league team and a works league team, e.g. Grange Moor, It is important that you select the correct team for the competition you are officiating in. Likewise, Kirklees Sunday league teams are prefixed by 'KSL' are are also located at the bottom of the drop down menu.

Compulsory questions are marked with a * and MUST be answered or you will not be able to submit your report. When you have answered all of the questions and you are happy that your report is complete, scroll to the bottom of the page and hit the submit button.
